Paul Rand Book Design

Project Scope

Using the text and images supplied in Paul Rand's Design and the Play Instinct, we were asked to develop a strong visual concept and utilize a grid system to create a book with constraints.

The use of grid was especially important so that the layout would be cohesive. I used imagery from the images given and have that inspire the layout of the text that followed since the emphasis of the text is on the importance of designs.
