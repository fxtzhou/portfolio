About Me

My aspirations in design came from wanting to solve problems and to create better and more compelling solutions.

When I interned abroad in Tokyo at ZigZaGame, I used those skills to create consistent graphics and adsets for their different game titles and marketing strategies. Adapting design to those strategies, intercommunicating with cross-functional teams, and collaborating were a part of my day-to-day.

My goal is to apply my knowledge obtained from both my education and international work experience towards expanding my horizons as a Designer and becoming an integral part of a team!
