Type Hierarchy Studies

Project Scope

Given a set of information, we were asked to explore compositions with certain constraints in the typeface Univers including font weight, font style, font size and rules. First all compositions were constructed through analog from cutting out pieces of paper and arranging them to get an idea of different compositions very quickly. Finally, all compositions were produced in inDesign.

This project really exercised the importance of understanding your given constraints and how to construct compositions effectively even with very limited room to explore.
