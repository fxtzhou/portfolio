Type Hierarchy Studies

Given a set of information, we were asked to explore compositions with certain constraints in the typeface Univers including font weight, font style, font size and rules. First all compositions were constructed through analog from cutting out pieces of paper and arranging them to get an idea of different compositions very quickly. Finally, all compositions were produced in inDesign.

This project really exercised the importance of understanding your given constraints and how to construct compositions effectively even with very limited room to explore. With each set, there were less and less constraints given but I found through my process that the constraints helped guide my designs, making them stronger each way. Thus, in Set 3, I continued to use much of the initial given constraints and explored background.


Set 1

  • One size/One weight (15 pt Univers 45)
  • One size/Two weights (15pt Univers 45 and 65)

Set 2

  • Two sizes/Two weights (15 pt/9pt Univers 45 and 65)
  • Two sizes/Two weights/Two styles ((15 pt/9pt Univers 45, 46, 65, and 66)

Set 3

  • Use paragraph rules
  • Any size, weight and style of Univers, with or without rules
